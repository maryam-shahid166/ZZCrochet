About Me

Hi, I'm Simrah, the person behind Z&Z Crochet, a biotechnologist by profession and a crocheter by passion. When I'm not working, you'll usually find me with a crochet hook in one hand and a ball of yarn in the other. What started as a creative escape has grown into a small handmade business where I create thoughtful, custom crochet pieces for people who appreciate something made with care. I make each piece in small batches, giving every order the attention it deserves. Along the way, I'm accompanied by my two curious cats, who take their roles as yarn inspectors very seriously.
